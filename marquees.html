Marquees

There are two types of marquees available, Structures and Free Standing, and a brief explanation of both is shown below. We have also provided for your information a guide showing the approximate seating and standing capacity for the various sized marquees. Please click here if you wish to view this document.
For prices on marquees, please contact us.

Clear Span Structures
Details
Range from 6m, 9m or 12m spans increasing in 3m or 5m increments. Stronger frame which is built on site.		 Extras
Silk lining, fairy lights, window or clear plastic walling, blank vinyl white walling, pendant lighting and flooring.		 Example(s)
All uses - weddings, corporate events, christenings, birthdays, engagements, parties, sporting.

Free Standing Marquees (Fixed Frame Style)
Details
3m x 3m or 6m spans increasing in 3m increments. Comes with canopy and optional walls. Stronger frame which is built on site.		 Extras
Pendant lighting can be added. 		Example(s)
Mainly used for backyard parties, school fetes and sporting carnivals.
